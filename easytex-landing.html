Our philosophy

Built by mathematicians who got tired of bad tools

"Mathematicians distrust black boxes. Any tool that hides the math isn't a tool—it's an obstacle."

We built EasyTeX because every existing tool fails in the same way: they prioritize accessibility over power, polish over transparency, and features over composability.

The result is software that treats mathematicians like beginners who need hand-holding. We think you deserve better. We think you deserve tools that respect your intelligence.

01

Transparency over automation

Show intermediate steps. Show assumptions. Show branching cases. Never hide the math.

02

Composability over features

Small, orthogonal primitives. Define, transform, prove, visualize, export. Build your own abstractions.

03

Density over decoration

If it feels like a research paper, we're doing it right. Semantic density. No gradients. No shadows.

04

Precision over friendliness

Precise language. Formal terminology. Explicit error messages. We respect your expertise.
